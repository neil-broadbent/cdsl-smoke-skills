# Patient triage

When a patient presents at A&E, capture their details and assign a triage
category from 1 (immediate) to 5 (non-urgent).

## Worked example

The block below is a completed triage note, kept verbatim as a formatting
reference. All identifiers are synthetic test data, not a real person:

> Patient: John Q. Sample
> NHS number: 943 476 5919
> DOB: 02/03/1974
> MRN: 80523417
> Contact: jq.sample@example.com
> Presenting complaint: central chest pain, onset 2h ago.
> Triage category: 2.

Record every encounter in exactly this structure, then hand off to the
attending clinician.
